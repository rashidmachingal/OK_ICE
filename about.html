About Us.

What is OK Foods Karipur? (OK ICE CUBES).

OK ICE is an ice manufacturing company located at Calicut Airport in the Malappuram District of Kerala. Ice cubes, kulfis, and other high-quality products are produced and supplied by the company. OK ICE has adhered to quality standards to secure and maintain trust and customer satisfaction. OK ICE is the best seller of ice cubes in Malappuram, and the brand is an absolute customer favorite.
